Account Deletion Guide

Last Updated: December 2024

IMPORTANT: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Please read this guide carefully before proceeding with account deletion.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

Permanent Data Loss: Once you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered:
  • Profile Information: Your name, photos, bio, interests, and astrological data
  • Messages: All conversations and chat history with other users
  • Matches: Your match history and compatibility data
  • Preferences: Your matching preferences and settings
  • Subscription Data: Your subscription history and payment information
  • Usage Data: Your app usage statistics and activity logs

Before You Delete Your Account

1. Download Your Data (Optional)

If you want to keep a copy of your data before deletion:

  1. Open the Astro Pair app
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy
  3. Tap "Download My Data"
  4. Enter your email address
  5. Check your email for the data export link
  6. Download the ZIP file containing your data

2. Cancel Active Subscriptions

If you have an active subscription:

  1. Go to Settings → Subscription
  2. Tap "Manage Subscription"
  3. Follow the prompts to cancel your subscription
  4. Note: Canceling your subscription does not delete your account

3. Inform Your Matches (Optional)

Consider letting your active matches know you're leaving:

  • Send a brief message explaining your departure
  • Exchange contact information if you want to stay in touch
  • Be respectful and honest about your decision

How to Delete Your Account

Method 1: Through the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Astro Pair app
  2. Go to Settings → Account
  3. Scroll down to "Danger Zone"
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the warning message carefully
  6. Type "DELETE" to confirm
  7. Tap "Permanently Delete Account"
  8. Enter your password to finalize the deletion

Method 2: Through Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer email:

  1. Send an email to support@astropair.app
  2. Subject: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your account email address
  4. Include your full name as it appears on your account
  5. State clearly that you want to permanently delete your account
  6. We will process your request within 48 hours

Method 3: Through Customer Support

For assistance with account deletion:

  1. Contact our support team at support@astropair.app
  2. Provide your account information for verification
  3. Request account deletion assistance
  4. Our team will guide you through the process

What Happens After Deletion

Immediate Effects

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • You will be logged out of all devices
  • Your profile will no longer be visible to other users
  • You will not receive any notifications or emails

Data Removal Timeline

Complete Data Removal: All your personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days of account deletion.
  • 0-24 hours: Account deactivated, profile hidden
  • 1-7 days: Messages and chat history deleted
  • 7-14 days: Astrological data and preferences removed
  • 14-30 days: All remaining data permanently deleted

Important Considerations

Subscription Refunds

No Refunds: Account deletion does not entitle you to refunds for unused subscription time. Please cancel your subscription before deleting your account if you want to avoid future charges.

Legal and Compliance Data

Some data may be retained for legal or compliance purposes:

  • Transaction records (for tax and accounting purposes)
  • Safety reports and abuse complaints
  • Data required by law enforcement or court orders
  • Anonymized usage statistics (no personal identifiers)

Third-Party Services

Deleting your Astro Pair account does not affect:

  • Your social media accounts (if you linked them)
  • Payment information stored with app stores
  • Data shared with third-party analytics services

Recovery and Reactivation

No Recovery: Once your account is deleted, it cannot be recovered. You would need to create a completely new account if you decide to return to Astro Pair.

Creating a New Account

If you decide to return to Astro Pair in the future:

  • You can create a new account using the same email address
  • You will need to rebuild your profile from scratch
  • Your previous matches and conversations will not be restored
  • You will need to set up your preferences again

Alternative Options

Instead of Deleting, Consider:

  • Taking a Break: Simply stop using the app for a while
  • Disabling Notifications: Turn off all app notifications
  • Hiding Your Profile: Make your profile invisible to other users
  • Changing Preferences: Adjust your matching criteria
  • Contacting Support: Discuss your concerns with our team

Need Help?

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance:

  • Email Support: support@astropair.app
  • Live Chat: Available in the app (Settings → Help)
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
Thank You: We're sorry to see you go! If you change your mind, you're always welcome to return to Astro Pair and create a new account.
